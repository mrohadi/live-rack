# 2. Getting Around (Navigation)

Every screen shares the same frame: a **sidebar** on the left, a **top bar**
across the top, and the **page** in the middle.

## 2.1 The sidebar

The left sidebar is your map of the whole app. It is grouped into three
sections that mirror how a warehouse actually works:

### Operate — the daily floor

| Item | Goes to | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Overview** | [Dashboard](11-analytics.md) | Today's key numbers at a glance |
| **Map & Zones** | [Map](03-map-and-zones.md) | The warehouse floor plan |
| **Scanner** | [Scanner](04-scanner.md) | Scan items in, out, and around |
| **Inventory** | [Inventory](05-inventory.md) | What's in stock, where |

### Workflows — getting orders out

| Item | Goes to | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Tasks** | [Tasks](06-tasks.md) | Jobs to be done |
| **Picking** | [Picking](07-picking.md) | Collect items for an order |
| **Waves** | [Waves](08-waves.md) | Pick many orders in one trip |
| **Dispatch** | [Dispatch](09-dispatch.md) | Pack and ship |
| **Pipelines** | [Pipelines](10-pipelines.md) | Custom multi-step workflows |

### Insights & Setup — the office

| Item | Goes to | Purpose |
|------|---------|---------|
| **Analytics** | [Analytics](11-analytics.md) | Trends, heatmaps, performance |
| **Integrations** | [Integrations](12-integrations.md) | Connect outside systems |
| **Users & Access** | [Users](13-users-and-access.md) | People, roles, security (Admin only) |

> Items you are not allowed to use are hidden. For example, **Users & Access**
> only appears for Admins.

## 2.2 The top bar

- **Store selector** ("Store #14") — switch warehouses. Changes every screen.
- **Search box** — type a SKU, zone, or task to jump straight to it. See below.
- **Density toggle** (Compact / Balanced / Roomy) — controls how tightly rows
  and cards are packed. Pick what is comfortable on your screen.
- **Notifications bell** — shows alerts addressed to you (task assignments,
  deadlines). A red dot means unread items. Click to open the panel; opening it
  marks them read.

## 2.3 Search

The search box understands **SKUs, zones, and tasks**. Start typing and pick a
result to go directly to that item's screen. Use it as a shortcut instead of
clicking through menus.

## 2.4 Notifications

When a task is assigned to you, or one of your tasks is due soon, a
notification appears under the bell. The list keeps your recent alerts so you
can catch up. Notifications are **personal** — you only see the ones meant for
you.

## 2.5 On mobile

On a phone, the sidebar collapses into a top menu and a bottom tab bar with the
most-used screens (Home, Map, Scan, Tasks, Items). Open the menu for the full
list.
